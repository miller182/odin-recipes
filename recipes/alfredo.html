Alfredo

Home

Alfredo sauce is a rich, creamy sauce made with butter and Parmesan cheese. It's a simple and elegant sauce that takes just a few minutes to make. You can use it to dress up pasta, seafood, vegetables, or meat. Alfredo sauce is best served immediately, as it may become grainy if reheated.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
  3. Stir in the Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
