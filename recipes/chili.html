Chili

Home

Chili is a hearty and flavorful stew made with ground beef, beans, and tomatoes. It's a popular dish in the United States, especially during the colder months. Chili can be made with a variety of ingredients, including different types of beans, vegetables, and spices. It's typically served with toppings like cheese, sour cream, and green onions. Chili is a great make-ahead dish, as it tastes even better the next day. It can be made on the stovetop, in a slow cooker, or in an Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15-ounce) can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (15-ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 (4-ounce) can diced green chilies
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon sugar

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, cook and stir ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add onions and garlic, and cook until onions are transparent.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, kidney beans, pinto beans, green chilies, beef broth, chili powder, cumin, salt, pepper, cayenne pepper, paprika, oregano, and sugar. Stir to combine.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Adjust seasonings to taste. Serve hot with desired toppings.
